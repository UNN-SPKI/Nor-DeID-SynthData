nb_health_care_units.csv
----
Lists healthcare providers that are part of, or have agreements with the national healthcare service in Norway
Source: Helsenorge.no
Provider name retrieved from the "name" field from
https://tjenester.helsenorge.no/proxy/VelgBehandlingssted/api/v1/Behandlingssteder
Retrieved 03.07.2023

nb_diagnoses_full_icd10.csv
----
Lists the full-length Norwegian names of diagnosis codes in the 2023 revision of ICD-10
Source: Direktoratet for e-helse
Diagnosis names from the "Tekst uten lengdebegrensning" field in 
https://www.ehelse.no/kodeverk-og-terminologi/ICD-10-og-ICD-11
Retrieved 04.07.2023

icd10cm-codes-April-1-2023.txt
----
Lists the full-length English names of diagnosis codes in the 2023 revision of ICD-10
Source: Centers for Disease Control and Prevention
ICD-10-CM FY 2023 April 1 2023 List of codes and Descriptions
https://www.cdc.gov/nchs/icd/Comprehensive-Listing-of-ICD-10-CM-Files.htm
Retrieved 04.07.2023

en_diagnoses_subset.csv

Lists a subset of ICD-10 diagnosis code names, generated by running filter-icd10.py
